What is "Name"

“Name" is a tool to help you learn better and more efficiently during online school. As current high school students ourselves, we have experienced the challenges posed by virtual classes. With hour long classes, it becomes hard to focus on what the teacher is saying for that long of a time, especially with the range of distractions at home.
That's why "Name" was created, to help you get the key ideas and concepts from your teacher’s lecture. Not only does “Name” generate a complete transcript of the audio, it also provides the best resources from across the internet to help you better understand what you’re learning.

How to Use

Head to the application page, which you can find in the navbar at the top of the page. Here you'll see 2 options. You can either upload your own audio file or proivde the title of a YouTube video that you want the transcript and key concepts for.
